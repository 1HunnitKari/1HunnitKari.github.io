Lemon Chicken

Ingredients:

14 oz. chicken breasts
    8 oz. chicken consome 2 tsp. garlic 2 tsp. olive oil 1 tsp. parsley, chopped 1 tsp. capers 1 tsp. butter 1 lemon, halved Instructions:

    Heat pan with olive oil. Sear chicken.
      Add garlic, lemon and consome to pan. Place in preheated 375F oven and bake 12-14 minutes. Remove chicken and lemon, add parsley and capers. Reduce liquid in pan until 4 tablespoons remain. Remove pan from heat, wait 1 minute and then add butter. Shake pan to blend. Pour sauce over chicken and serve. Servings: 2
